REVENUE SHARING MODEL WITH GAME DEVELOPERS

Game developers face challenges with outdated models like advertising and in-app purchases, and fierce market competition. Playtoo changes this by allowing developers to focus on creating engaging games while we handle logistics, advertising, and entertainment models like tournaments and challenges. Developers can keep their games on other platforms without restrictions, benefiting from Web3 while still participating in Web2. Additionally, 80% of platform revenues go to winning players as prize pools, 10% to game developers, and 10% stays with Playtoo, ensuring developers receive the recognition they deserve. By 2024, 40% of Web2 game developers/studios aim to move to Web3

COMPETITION: TOURNAMENTS - 1VS1 - MATCH MULTIPLAYERS

Playtoo transforms beloved Web2 games into monetizable opportunities through various competitions. Depending on the type of game, it can be monetized through tournaments, 1vs1 challenges, or team multiplayer battles. This feature enables game developers to significantly increase their earnings by placing their games on Playtoo for free, seamlessly transitioning into the Web3 era and unlocking a new revenue stream.
