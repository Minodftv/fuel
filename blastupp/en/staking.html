Staking info
  • LockedBLASTUP is a special token that is pegged to the $BLASTUP token rate.
  • LockedBLASTUP will be distributed to all $BLASTUP holders on the Blast network based on the snapshot of balances from May 31, 2024.
  • LockedBLASTUP can be staked to earn additional rewards: increased APR and bonus Booster Points.
  • The longer you stake, the higher the APR reward. After ending stake period reward will stop accumulate
  • To participate in the IDO, you must stake a minimum of 2,000 LockedBLASTUP tokens.
  • At the TGE, you can convert LockedBLASTUP tokens to $BLASTUP tokens at a 1:1 ratio.
